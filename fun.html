More about me!

Apart from studying and drawing, I have a few other interests as well :D

Scuba Diving

I can't describe exactly the feeling I had when I was surrounded on all sides by ocean, fish and kelp - as if I was just an observer peering inside this whole different world.

One interesting thing I learned: because blue light has more energy than other colors of visible light, it penetrates the deepest. As a result, the deeper I got, the more blue my surroundings became.

We got to see some astonishingly large fish; the tuna in this photo was approximately as long as my brother was tall (he's the one in the middle). For reference, my brother is 5' 11".
